You have the fire of music within you, and all you need is an instrument to express it. 

It could be the guitar if you like taking centre-stage, the bansuri if you like soaking in calmness, the cello for when you feel effortlessly bold, or any of a dozen other instruments. 

You might even want to play all of these. And you can.

In any case, the goal is not just to play your instrument, but to forget that you're playing it altogether. 

That's when an instrument feels like an extension of your being.
