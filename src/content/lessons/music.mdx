If you're not satisfied with simply imitating music, then understanding it like a language might just be what you're looking for. 

This doesn't mean learning music theory, but actively listening to and speaking musical sound. 

Starting with recognising and recalling the music you hear, you can build up to fluency in your favourite musical style, whether it's Indian raaga music, Western harmony, pop, rock, or hip-hop. 

It all starts with intentional listening, and the fluency that arises is what leads to expressive composition and improvisation: the marks of masterful musicianship.
