Your words already convey meaning, but maybe you're looking to communicate more: your unique perspective on the world. 

But how do you communicate something so subjective using a shared vocabulary? It's not just about the words, but also how you put them together. 

With poetic phrasing, learn how to use the intentional arrangement of sounds and rhythms to elevate language into an art. 

Without music, you have poetry. With music, a song.
