The First Pass

In this part of the Melody series, we’ll decode the loudness contours of the 5 melodies we heard in the preceding part.

We’ll be reusing the same plan and tools that we used to decode the children’s rhyme in Part 25, the only change being that we’ll complete every stage of the plan with all 5 melodies before moving on to the next stage.

The first stage is decoding the loudness contour. If you need a refresher on loudness, check out the Melodic Loudness Primer.

The Gear You’ll Need

To make it easier to focus on the loudness levels of each melody’s pitches, we’ll turn every pitch into the same Sa, following a slower tempo1.

This modified version, along with the provided loudness dictionary, will be all you’ll need to name the loudness level of each pitch (Ve, Ya or Ha).

Loud, Soft and In Between

Decode the loudness contours of these modified versions of the 5 melodies.

  • Melody 1: Netru Illaatha Maatram (approximately 0:28-0:40 of the linked video), A.R. Rahman

    Hint:

    Ha Ya _ Ha _ _, Ha _ _, _ _ _,

    Ya _ Ya _ _ _, _ _ _, _ Ya Ha.

  • Melody 2: Jab Se Tumi San Laagali (approx. 1:25-1:36 of linked video), traditional Khayaal composition

    Hint:

    Ha Ya _ _ Ya Ya _ Ya, Ha _ _,

    _ _ _ Ha _ _ Ya, _ _ _ _,

    _ _ Ya, _ _ _ Ya Ya.

  • Melody 3: Symphony No. 25 (approx. 1:34-1:46 of linked video), W.A. Mozart

    Hint:

    Ve Ve Ve, _ _ _, _ _ _, Ve Ve _ _,

    _ _ _, _ _ _, _ _ _, _ _ Ve Ve,

    Ve _ Ya _ Ya Ve _ _,

    _ _ Ya _ Ya _ Ve Ve,

    _ _ _ _ _ Ya _ Ya,

    Ha _ _ Ha _.

  • Melody 4: Pehla Nasha (approx. 0:47-1:00 of linked video), Jatin-Lalit

    Hint:

    Ha Ya Ha, _ _ _ _ _,

    Ha _ Ha, _ _ _ _ Ya,

    _ Ya _, Ya Ya _ _ _,

    Ha _ _ _,

    _ _ _ _ _ _ _ Ha.

  • Melody 5: Mamma Mia (approx. 1:03-1:20 of linked video), ABBA

    Hint:

    Ya, Ha Ha _, _ _ _ _,

    Ya, _ _ _, _ _ _ _,

    _, _ Ha, _ _ _ Ya _ _ _ Ya,

    Ha Ha _ _, _ Ve _,

    _ _ Ha Ha Ha _,

    _, _ _, Ha _ _ _, _ _ _ Ha.
