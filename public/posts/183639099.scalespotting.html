Tri-Level

It’s time to apply our scale (and melody) skills!

There are 3 levels of difficulty in this concluding part of the Scale series:

  • Level I: Scale recognition,

  • Level II: Scalar pitch recognition, and

  • Level III: Melodic pitch recognition.

The premise of Level I is simple: listen to 3 short melodies, and match each of them to the appropriate scale.

Level II takes it a step (or 3) further and asks you to identify the pitches in each scale.

Level III is the most demanding, requiring you to decode the pitch sequence of every melody. If you need help understanding how to do that, the Decoding Melody series is your friend:

There solutions are at the end of this post, but if you want to know how it feels to apply what you’ve gained from this series, I’d strongly recommend giving each level a fair shot by yourself first :)
