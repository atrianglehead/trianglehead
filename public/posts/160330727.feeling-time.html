Let’s try to recognise chunks of time, just by listening.

We’ve visually decoded the rhythm of The Melody, and learnt a lot about rhythm along the way, but we haven’t yet tried decoding rhythms by ear.

It’s time to try our hand at the real thing, through 10 challenges spread across 3 levels of difficulty.

If you’re fuzzy about any aspect of our rhythmic journey so far, now would be a great time to do a quick review, beginning with Part 9 and going all the way upto Part 13.

All the best!

What to Expect

We’re going to keep things simple by using only 3 sub-rhythms — or phrases — in various combinations. Each challenge (in any level) is basically some combination of these 3 phrases.

These phrases are:

  • Phrase 1: Ta,

  • Phrase 2: TaKa, and

  • Phrase 3: TaMi.

In Level I, these 3 phrases will each have a unique sound. This way, it’ll be easy to recognise them.

In Level II, the phrases will sound more alike, without sounding identical. You’ll need to pay more attention to the timing of the inner beats in this level.

In Level III, sound differences will vanish and the only way to recognise each phrase will be based on the timing of the inner beats.

In every rhythm challenge, tempo-beats — essentially, Ta — will be explicitly part of the rhythm.
