Calibrate Your Device’s Volume

Before we begin, let’s calibrate our device volumes. Listen to the following sound:

Set your device volume to a level where you can clearly hear this sound without it being painful or uncomfortable.

We’re doing this to ensure that there are no arbitrary changes to loudness across challenges.

Once you’ve set the volume to a comfortable level, let it stay the same until after the last challenge.

What to Expect

To keep it relatively easy, we’ll restrict our loudness options to just Ve (soft) and Ya (loud).

Level I will use just one pitch with loudness Ya and Ve.

Level II will use two pitches with loudness Ya and Ve.

Level III will use three pitches with loudness Ya and Ve.

All you need to do is decode the loudness of each pitch that you hear. There is no need to decode the pitches themselves.

Each new level introduces an additional pitch for added challenge. You’ll need to ignore changes in pitch and only focus on changes in loudness.
